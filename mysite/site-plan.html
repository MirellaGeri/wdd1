Content

HOME PAGE

On my website, I will show the profile of a young woman who does many art projects such as crocheting, paper puppets, drawings, character designs, etc. We want her profile to be known to all those who like to see art and also to be able to meet her and her creations everywhere.

Images for the Home page

ABOUT

Visitors will know details of the person who will be talked about on the website because it will have a page where they will be posted, and some contact information if they want to chat on Instagram, Facebook, or email, there is also the possibility to receive sponsors to do more new art projects or also if a company wants to hire their services and buy her arts.

Images for the Page 2

CROCHET

One of the web pages will show in detail a collection of crochet works of art with some summarized data on how to make them for those who want to learn as beginners, it will show all the designs that the creator has made so far and will upload the new designs when she finishes making them.
