ABOUT ME

My name is Geraldine Martinez Salcedo. I was born and raised in Lima, Peru. When I was a child, my dream was to become a pastry chef. Along the way, I developed some talents that I love. I started drawing, crocheting, and making crafts.

I consider myself a versatile artist who likes to work on all kinds of craft projects like crochet keychains, paper or cardboard pencil holders, or character design. I love color, textures, and shapes and enjoy creating them with different materials. My new dream is to become a graphic designer and to create stories for my series.

When I am not drawing or making any crafts I enjoy the normal things: watching videos, taking a walk with my sister, and spending time with my family. I love, watching my favorite YouTubers, and listening to my favorite list of music.
